# Facebook Page Growth Checklist
**Current:** 125 followers  
**Target:** 500 followers  
**Goal:** Unlock monetization eligibility

> Quick open: `/resources/facebook-growth`
---

## Real Talk First

You don't need "viral hacks." You need **consistent basics executed for 4–6 weeks.**  
The algorithm favors active pages with real engagement — not pages with bots or paid followers.

At 125 followers, you already have an audience. The work now is **showing up reliably so they share you, and more people find you.**

---

## Week 1–2 Foundation (Do this first, don't skip)

**Page Optimization**
- [ ] Profile photo is clean & recognizable at thumbnail size
- [ ] Cover photo states what you do in ≤ 5 words
- [ ] About section (first 155 chars) includes keywords people search for
- [ ] CTA button set to "Follow" or "Send Message"
- [ ] Page category set correctly (not "just for fun" — use your actual content type)

**Posting Setup**
- [ ] Aim for 3–4 posts per week (Reels > static posts — Reels get 2–5× more reach)
- [ ] Schedule posts via Meta Creator Studio (free) at 2–3 PM your time
- [ ] Drop a Story daily, even if it's 5 seconds ("new post coming tomorrow")

---

## Daily Habits (10–15 min/day)

- [ ] Reply to every comment within 2 hours of posting
- [ ] React to Stories from your most engaged followers
- [ ] Post at least 1 Story daily (text, poll, or behind-the-scenes)

---

## Weekly Focus (Do these intentionally)

### Content (3–4 posts/week)
Make each post do ONE of these:
- [ ] **Teach something** – "3 tools that changed my workflow"
- [ ] **Take a stand** – disagree with common advice (respectfully)
- [ ] **Show process** – behind-the-scenes, "how it's made"
- [ ] **Ask a question** – ends with "Drop a comment if you've tried this"

**Format rule:** Reels first. If you're not making Reels yet, start this week — shortform video is the single highest-leverage format right now.

### Engagement (20–30 min total)
- [ ] Find 5–10 Facebook Groups in your niche
- [ ] Comment thoughtfully in 2–3 groups per day (not "check out my page")
- [ ] DM 2–3 of your most engaged followers: "What content do you want to see next?"
- [ ] Ask 1 follower per week: "Will you share my latest post? No pressure."

### Cross-Platform Seeding
- [ ] Post the same content to Instagram, TikTok, and/or YouTube Shorts concurrently
- [ ] Each post links back to your Facebook Page in bio/caption

## Follower Milestone Rewards (Optional but keeps you honest)

- [ ] 150: First milestone — reward yourself something small
- [ ] 200: Start a monthly Q&A post
- [ ] 300: Unofficial shoutout to your top 5 followers
- [ ] 400: Ask the audience what they want next (they'll tell you)
- [ ] 500: Monetization eligibility unlocked — apply for in-stream ads / Stars / fan subscriptions

---

## What NOT To Do

- [ ] Don't buy followers or engagement (Facebook detects this and hurts your reach)
- [ ] Don't post and ghost — engagement in the first 2 hours matters most
- [ ] Don't copy viral trends blindly — trend-hopping without relevance confuses your audience
- [ ] Don't delete "underperforming" posts — they still show you're active

---

## When You Hit 500

Facebook's eligibility generally requires:
- 500 followers  
- 60-day Page history  
- 60,000 1-minute views OR 10M Reel plays in last 60 days  
- Comply with Partner Monetization Policies  

Once you hit 500, apply via: **Meta Business Suite → Monetization**
